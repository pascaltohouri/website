My goal is to quantify welfare and contribute to human flourishing. Intelligent systems offer the most promising method for solving societal welfare problems. So, I am building a series of models (called Ethos 0.x) to help achieve this goal.

My research pipeline goes:

  • 01. Blog post (here)
  • 02. Pre-print output (Figshare)
  • 03. Code (GitHub)
  • 04. Publication (arXiv / Journals)
