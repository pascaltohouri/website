Pascal Tohouri

My name is Pascal, and my goal is to quantify welfare and contribute to human flourishing. Intelligent systems offer the most promising method for solving societal welfare problems at scale. So, I am currently building a series of models (called Ethos 0.x) that will help achieve this goal.

My research process goes:

  • Blog post (here),
  • Pre-prints (Fighshare),
  • Code (Github),
  • Publication (arXiv / external hosting).

So, you'll find many incipient ideas in these blog posts. For more polished work, please see the header links.
